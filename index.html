About Me

My Name is Emily Jonaitis. I am a very driven person. I love skateboarding and I feel like skateboarding in a lot of ways is like coding. If you don't know how to do something, you go to other sources on tips and tricks on how to do that certain thing.

Then you practice and practice and continue to try until you get the overall answer or result that you want. You might take a few falls here and there and thats good! It all comes with learning something new.

At the end once you land that trick or have the result that you were striving towards. You have a sense of accomplishment like no other. Which feels amazing and just makes you want to learn more.

I have certainly enjoyed making this website!
